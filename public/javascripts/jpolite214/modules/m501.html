Label & ID

Go to UL#header_tabs section of index.html to customize the label and ID of each tab:

<ul id="header_tabs">
	<li id="t1">About V2</li>
	<li id="t2">Layout</li>
	<li id="t3">Module</li>
	<li id="t4">Controls</li>
	<li id="t5">Customize</li>
	<li id="t6">Messaging</li>
...

Naming of the IDs are just arbitrary, as long as they make good sense to you :)

Look & Feel & Behavior

Visual styles of the tabs can be altered in style.css under #header_tabs *

As you've already experienced when opening this page, you can choose among 3 options to customize behavior of the tabs, namely, 'Kwicks, 'LavaLamp' and 'Traditional'. While 'Traditional' is just a simple native implementation with tricks from Dragon Interactive, the other two are jQuery user interface plugins integrated directly without modification.

So for your system, you can pick one tab style given above or select another plugin from the jQuery plugin diretory.

When you switch to another plugin, do follow the plugin guidelines to properly set the CSS styles of tab items.
